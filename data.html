How to use our data

Our data is served primarily through an S3 bucket. You are allowed to consume this file; it may act as your backend if you wish.

Reporting data issues

We have exposed a web service for reporting data issues. Please refer to the API documentation for more information.

Submitting new data

There are two ways to submit new data:

Batch upload

In order to submit data by batch upload, please email us and include the data in a tabular format, and our volunteers will take care of this. Alternatively, you may connect with us directly using the Discord server.
